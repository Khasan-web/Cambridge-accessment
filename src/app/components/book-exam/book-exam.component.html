Cambridge Exam Application Form

IMPORTANT INFORMATION

Exam places are given on a first-come-first-served basis. Your place cannot be guaranteed until we receive full payment of the exam fee.

The exam is organised in 2 parts – Speaking and Paper-Based – these will take place on 2 separate dates. You must sit all components of the exam in order to qualify for your result.

You must be available between the dates in red for the speaking examination. You will be notified of the exact date later, no later than 2 weeks in advance. No refund will be given if you become unavailable.

Exam fees cannot be refunded except in cases of serious illness or bereavement. Documentation must be provided.

You must bring photographic identification (passport or national ID card) with you on the day of your exam. You will also have a photograph taken on the day of the Speaking exam.

Please note that online results will be available 4-6 weeks after the Paper-Based exam date. Occasionally, certificates take up to 3 months to arrive at IH Aberdeen. Once they have arrived, we will post them to the address you have given on your exam registration form. Please contact IH Aberdeen directly if you change address.

Please read the terms and conditions on the exam registration form carefully before signing it and completing payment.
