About Us

We are an Authorized Exam Centre by Cambridge Assessment English. The convenient location of Edu-Action at the Centre of the Tashkent near the Metro Oybek allows us to provide comfortable conditions for taking the exam. The main principle of our centre is a client-oriented approach. Candidates can register online and as well as in our office, where our friendly members of staff are always ready to give you full support.

Along with offering the Cambridge tests we offer wide range of English language learning assist, Cambridge preparation and practice materials. Moreover, Edu-Action is an accredited IELTS exam ( www.exams.uz ) Centre by IDP Australia, and also is an approved CELTA training Centre and provides consultancy on STUDY ABROAD program.
