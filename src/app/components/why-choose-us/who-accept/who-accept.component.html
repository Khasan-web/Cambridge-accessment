Why choose Cambridge English Qualifications

Cambridge English Qualifications are in-depth exams, each of which focuses on a level of the Common European Framework of Reference (CEFR). By accepting Cambridge English Qualifications you can:

  • Be confident that applicants have the right English skills to succeed

    • Choose exams for your admission requirements that are set at the right level for academic success. Cambridge English exams test all language skills – reading, writing, listening and speaking – with speaking tested face-to-face.

  • Increase the number of high-quality applicants to your institution

    • Accept our exams as part of your admissions criteria and you will be able to select from thousands of high-calibre candidates from around the world.

  • Diversify your international student population

    • Improve your institution’s profile internationally and attract candidates from beyond your usual range of countries.
